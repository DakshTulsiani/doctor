Healthcare Access For Everyone

Have you ever had to repeat a medical test simply because you visited a different doctor who didn't have access to your previous records? With HealthAccess, that hassle is a thing of the past. Our platform allows authorized healthcare providers across India to securely access your medical records using just your registered email address. Click below to learn more about how we're making healthcare simpler and more connected.

Our Goal

One Goal, One Solution

Our goal is to create a website and app where only licensed doctors can enter patients' medical records on any electronic device. They start by opening the website, entering the patient's email address, and confirming it with an OTP sent to that email. This allows access to the patient's previous medical records, preventing the repetition of already conducted tests. For the current visit, the doctor fills out an online health form for the patient, which is saved to the website under the same registered email. Additionally, the doctor can generate a printout to provide a hard copy to the patient, including the prescription for their current record.
